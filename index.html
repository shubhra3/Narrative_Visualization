Note:
The data used for all charts in this visualization is sourced in real-time from the World Bank Development Indicators (WDI) Dataset using the organization's APIs. No pre-loaded data files (.csv, .xlsx, etc.) are used, and D3.js is the data visualization tool used for this interactive Sceneshow. The indicators of access to electricity for urban and rural populations (denoted in the dataset as EG.ELC.ACCS.UR.ZS and EG.ELC.ACCS.RU.ZS, respectively) measure the proportion of people with reliable access to electricity, as a percentage of total aggregated population of the urban or rual areas within the queried country/region. Similarly, the indicators of access to sanitation for urban and rural populations (denoted in the dataset as SH.STA.BASS.UR.ZS and SH.STA.BASS.RU.ZS, respectively) measure the proportion of people with reliable access to sanitation. Finally, the indicators of access to clean water for urban and rural populations (denoted in the WDI dataset as SH.H2O.BASW.UR.ZS and SH.H2O.BASW.UR.ZS, respectively) measure the proportion of people with reliable access to clean water, as percentages of total aggregated populations of the urban or rual areas within queried country/region.
